Google Santa Tracker
The Google Santa Tracker is an annual Christmas-themed entertainment website offers educational content includes a collection of fun games related to holiday traditions, geography, and various cultures around the world. This content is designed to be informative and engaging, providing valuable learning opportunities for users of all ages. It includes a countdown timer that builds excitement by tracking the days, hours, and minutes until Santa's departure on Christmas Eve. Explore and play with Santa's elves all December long.
