Oskar Fischinger

Fullscreen

Description

The Google Doodle Fischinger Game launched Jun 22, 2017 to celebrate Oskar Fischinger’s 117th Birthday. The game captures the spirit of his innovative and experimental approach to art and provides a fun and engaging way for people to explore his unique aesthetic. Players can use the mouse or touch screen to draw lines and shapes that generate different sounds and patterns, allowing them to experiment with different combinations and create their own unique pieces of music.
